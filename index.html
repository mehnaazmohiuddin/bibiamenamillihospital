About Us

Bibia Menami Milli Hospital is committed to providing exceptional healthcare services to our community. With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced medical professionals, we ensure the highest standards of patient care. Established 25 years ago by our esteemed late Chairman Janab Moinuddin Ghori, our hospital embodies a vision to provide affordable healthcare in the Chandrayan Gotta area, catering to the diverse needs of the Old City population across various specialties. Rooted in core values of Excellence, Affordability, and a Humane touch, we extend our services to over 60 families free of cost, particularly those under the poverty line dealing with Diabetes and Hypertension. Monthly check-ups and a month's supply of free medicines are provided with the support of FAIZ-E-AAM TRUST Hyderabad. Mr. Iftekhar Husain, the secretary of Faiz e Aam, personally contributes nearly Rs. 50,000 monthly to run our Academy of Nursing and Para-Medical Sciences. Not only are the educational services provided free of cost, but scholarships are also granted to every student. To date, we have educated more than 3500 students in various evidence-based nursing and paramedical courses.
